Hiking

Hiking

There are many options for beach walks- all best around low tide. There is a tide book in living room on round table for reference.

Salmon Creek beach is just a few minutes from cabin door. It is a couple miles long and offers a lovely long stroll. This is a very popular beach for surfers that tend to congregate near mouth of the creek/north end of beach.

Shell Beach (8 minute drive north from cabin) is the trailhead for several hikes and there is map at the parking lot that illustrates the trails. You can mix a walk along the bluffs with loop back along the beach. Shortest loop is 2 miles- this is trail south from the parking lot. Longest is about 6.5 round trip to Red Hill (Pomo Campground). Option is to either start at Shell Beach or Pomo Campground. We like to start at Pomo as it gives option of circling back at half way point if full loop seems too ambitious. This walk provides spectacular views of the Russian River and the coast. It is a mix of redwoods, bay trees and open space- also prime for picnic lunch. This is also great option if it is windy or high tide.

Pinnacle Gulch

Best option for tide pools-and because of this, definitely best at low tide. Drive south 5 minutes to Bodega Harbor (development with golf course just south of town). As you pull up drive, you will see signs to Pinnacle Gulch. There is parking (fee of $7) at lot. The walk takes you down the gulch to the beach. Once there head south/left and you will cross over a couple of rocky areas. After the second rocky patch, you will see another trail- Shorttail Gulch which makes a 3 mile loop.

Thanks to Mary from Knix's Cabin for the recommendations.

Around

Explore

The coast north of Bodega Bay is scenic and wild. If you want to relax for a couple days, look at Sea Ranch near Gualala. Further north, Mendocino has dramatic redwood forests.

The drive on Highway 1 coming from the Bay Area is beautiful. Tamales Bay, Point Reyes and the drive up the Marin County coast all have lots of great vistas and hikes.

Wine country is also right next door.
